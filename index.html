Dallas flight note

Fly into Anchorage, rent one car, and let the scenery do the work.

Dallas to Anchorage is roughly 3,050 miles, usually 7 to 8 hours nonstop or 8 to 10 hours with a connection. This planner assumes you start the driving portion after arrival in Anchorage.
